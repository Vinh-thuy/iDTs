Why Simulation Insight?

Context & Challenges:

  • Fragmented visibility: IT teams work with multiple monitoring and reporting tools, but without a unified view.
  • Limited anticipation: Existing tools report incidents, but few can explain root causes or predict future degradations.
  • No linkage between organizational responsibility, infrastructure, and activity-level metrics (performance, changes, incidents).

Strategic Opportunity:

  • Existing foundation with two projects: Eureka (chatbot LLM for IT ops) and Insight Booster (Org Knowledge Graph)
  • Mature infrastructure KG already available and usable
  • Abundance of available data (especially from ServiceNow, Discovery, Dynatrace, logs...) — but underexploited in an integrated reasoning system
  • New generation of intelligent agents (LLM + symbolic AI + predictive models) allows contextual reasoning over graphs
Simulation Insight emerges to bridge these silos — transforming the IT landscape from fragmented and reactive to interconnected and proactive.

What is Simulation Insight?

Our Goal: Build a Smart and Operational Simulation Capability

  • Unify three levels of knowledge in a living simulation layer:
    • Infrastructure Graph (topology, systems, dependencies)
    • Organizational Graph (roles, accountability – via Insight Booster)
    • Activity Graph (monitoring, changes, incidents – via Dynatrace, ServiceNow)
  • Enable natural language interaction to:
    • Understand and reformulate user intent (via Eureka)
    • Decompose and reason through questions (agentic logic, symbolic AI)
    • Trigger predictive analysis or scenario simulation (neural models)
  • Answer complex IT questions, such as:
    • "What would happen if I decommission this cluster next month?"
    • "Which services are systematically impacted by this degraded link?"
    • "Who should act first if availability drops by 20%?"
  • Deliver value early: even the first versions improve visibility, responsibility mapping, and user empowerment via Eureka.
Simulation Insight is the missing layer between data, intelligence, and operational decision-making — structured, explainable, and action-oriented.

Target Organization & Staffing

Unified and Synergistic Team Structure

    Simulation Insight is built upon a centralized team model that consolidates all required expertise in one place to maximize agility and delivery speed.

  • The initiative brings together two squads:
    • AI Core Function: leads innovation, experimentation, and prototyping of intelligent components (enriched graphs, reasoning agents, AI models).
    • AI Function Exposition: industrializes validated components and integrates them into production, notably via the Eureka assistant.
  • People: The co-location of skills from data science to DevOps fosters continuous exchange, rapid iteration, and a direct path from idea to deployment.
  • Technologies: Discovery (infrastructure scan), Open Data, Dynatrace and ServiceNow (real-time operational signals).
A true accelerator: by concentrating talent, tools, and intelligence in one integrated structure, Simulation Insight ensures alignment, agility, and measurable impact.

Functional Steps (How)

  1. I visualize the infrastructure: automated, relational mapping
  2. I link responsibilities: visibility into who owns what
  3. I make activity visible: incidents, performance, changes
  4. I query with Eureka: natural language, context, and understanding
  5. I simulate scenarios: anticipate impacts before deployment
  6. I get recommendations: decision support through hybrid AI
Value is delivered at every stage. A living, intelligent system in service of IT governance.
